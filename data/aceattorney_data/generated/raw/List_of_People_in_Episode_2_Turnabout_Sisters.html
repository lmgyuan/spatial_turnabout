Episode 2 opens with a phone call between Mia and her little sister Maya. Mia needs Maya to hold on to some evidence for her and arranges for her to come to the Fey & Co. Law offices later that evening to pick it up. However, someone else arrives at the office before Maya, and attempts to take the evidence (which is hidden inside "The Thinker" statue/clock). Somehow he knows about the evidence and kills Mia in order to keep her quiet. Maya arrives at the office as she had agreed, only to find her sister dead. Maya is discovered at the scene of the crime and is accused of the murder.

Case Information[edit | edit source]

  • Time of Crime: September 5, 9:00 PM
  • Location: Fey & Co. Law Offices, Mia's Office
  • Defendant: Maya Fey
  • Victim: Mia Fey
  • Cause of Death: Blunt force trauma to the head
  • Murder weapon: Statue Clock ("The Thinker")
  • Defense Attorney: Phoenix Wright
  • Prosecution Lawyer: Miles Edgeworth

Characters[edit | edit source]

Defense Attorney[edit | edit source]

Phoenix Wright

Phoenix was Mia's student. The reason he would defend Maya is that no one else would. Why? Can he defeat a prosecution lawyer with a four-year winning streak?

Defendant[edit | edit source]

Maya Fey

Maya Fey is Mia's younger sister and has been accused of her murder.

Victim[edit | edit source]

Mia Fey

Mia was a defense attorney just like Phoenix. She was Phoenix's boss and owned the office where the crime took place. Mia was investigating something, and the investigation had something to do with the murder, but what was it?

Prosecution Lawyer[edit | edit source]

Miles Edgeworth

He became a prosecutor at the age of 20. He has a profound hatred for crime and criminals. Many rumors surround Edgeworth and his willingness to do anything to achieve a Guilty verdict. Many believe he is a genius; many think he is corrupt.

Witness[edit | edit source]

April May

April May is a witness to the murder. She was in the Gatewater Hotel when she saw the murder take place. A well-endowed, flirty young woman with pink hair.

Bellboy

The bellboy is an employee at the Gatewater Hotel. He is a very, very strange character.

Other[edit | edit source]

Detective Dick Gumshoe

Gumshoe is absentminded and can be short-tempered at times. Mostly he is a friendly and helpful character. He has great respect for Miles Edgeworth.

Marvin Grossberg

Marvin Grossberg was Mia's mentor and is a defense attorney. Maya was told to ask him for help if she was ever in trouble, however, he is unable to assist her in this case. He has a highly recognizable throat clearing sound.

Redd White

Redd White is the CEO of Bluecorp. He appears to be very powerful, but what is his connection to the case?

Locations[edit | edit source]

  • Fey & Co. Law Offices
    This is the first room you see as you walk into Mia's office.

  • Office
    This is where the murder took place. You see Mia's body on the left, and everywhere else looks as though it has been thoroughly searched.

  • Detention Center
    This is where the defendant (in this case Maya) is being held.

  • April's Hotel Room
    This is where April claims to have witnessed the murder.

  • Marvin Grossberg's Office
    Grossberg works here as the name states. There is a fabulous painting on the wall.

  • Bluecorp
    This appears to be a very expensive room. Everything looks like it belongs to a very rich person.
