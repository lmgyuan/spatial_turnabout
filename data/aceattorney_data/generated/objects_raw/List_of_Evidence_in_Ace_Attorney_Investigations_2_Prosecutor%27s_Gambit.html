Turnabout Trigger[]

Prosecutor's Badge
Proof of my profession. However, I prefer to keep it in my pocket.


Newspaper Article
A special report about President Di-Jun Wang. Click here for details.


Steel Samurai Balloon
Started out floating to the right of the lakeside stage, but has since been popped.


Ms. Lloyd's Tape
A recording of the president's speech. Two gunshots can clearly be heard.


→ Bullet Trajectory
Extrapolations from bullet trajectories suggest that the two shots were fired from the left side of the spectator area.
↳Extrapolations from bullet trajectories suggest that Knight fired the shots that popped the balloon.


→ Security Plan
A diagram of the president's security arrangements. Almost certainly classified.
↳A diagram of the president's security arrangements, which were changed only yesterday.


Revolver → Assassin's Revolver → Mr. Knight's Revolver
Found in a trash can on the left side of the spectator area. Fitted with a laser sight. Two spent bullets.
↳Found in a trash can on the left side of the spectator area. Fitted with a laser sight. Two spent bullets. Used in the attempt.
↳Found in a trash can on the left side of the spectator area. Two spent bullets. Same model as the victim's.
↳Switched for the one found in the spectator area. Bullet markings match those of the bullet fired in the incident


→ Kay's Photograph → Kay's Photographs
A photograph of the president at the exact moment the incident occurred. Click here for details.
↳Three photographs taken before, during and after the shooting.


Red Raincoat
Seemingly discarded by the person in the red hood. Found under the Samurai Dog stand.
↳Missing a button from the right sleeve. Its wearer has an injury on the left side of their body.
↳There's blood on the inside. The person who was wearing it must have an injured left arm.


Security Monitors
Screens showing footage from in and around the aircraft. One monitor is missing.


Bulletproof Attaché Case
Designed to provide protection from gunfire. Knight and Rook were each issued one.


Crime Scene Notes
The victim bled to death from a gunshot wound to the torso. The bullet passed clean through his body.


Bulletproof Vest
Stopped the bullet that passed through the victim. The bullet itself was crushed flat by the impact.
↳A prop used in the staged assassination attempt. The flattened bullet was also planted ahead of time.


Calling Card
Left behind by the assassin Shelly de Killer when he made his escape. Adorned with his distinctive seashell emblem.


Bloody Bullet
Found in a security monitor. This seems to be the bullet that killed Rook.


The Captive Turnabout[]

Prosecutor's Badge
Proof of my profession. However, I prefer to keep it in my pocket.


Building Layout
A map of the facility, including the detention center and the prison proper.


Mr. Knight's Body
Cause of death: Stab wound to the throat. Death would have been instant. There is also a small cut on his scalp from when he suffered a blow to the head.
↳Killed instantly by a stab wound to the throat. The body is covered in dirt. There is also a small cut on his scalp from when he suffered a blow to the head.
↳Killed instantly by a stab wound to the throat. There is also a small cut on his scalp from when he suffered a blow to the head. A sweet scent can be detected.


Pocket Chess Set
A folding chessboard with the pieces stored inside. It appears to have belonged to the victim.
↳A folding chessboard with the pieces stored inside. It belonged to Knight, and was brought to him at his request by Saint.
↳Portable folding chessboard. Belonged to Knight. There is a hidden space inside the chessboard.
↳Knight hid a chisel in here to prevent it from being found by the prison staff.


Rubber Glove
Only one glove was found at the crime scene. Prison-issued equipment for working inmates. Stained with blood and mud.
↳Found in Workshop A. Used by Sahwit when he was doing his animal styling training. Stained with blood and mud.
↳Found in Workshop A. Sullied after being worn by Sahwit when administering a dark mudpack treatment. Also bloodstained.


Rope
Discovered on top of Knight's body. Does this mean the victim was tied up?


Bloodstained Sheet
Colorful tarp found by the victim's body. Some of these were being stored in Workshop A.


Door Sensors
Fitted to each workshop's door, they detect and react to the prisoners' bracelets by raising the alarm.
↳They open the doors if an animal is detected, but raise the alarm if an inmate's bracelet is detected. Prisoners are not allowed to move about freely.


Prison Roll Call
Held three times a day: 7 AM, noon, and 9 PM. The victim's body had not been found at the time of the 7 AM roll call.


Black Dog
Sahwit claims to have seen this dog biting the victim's throat. He doesn't seem to like me very much.


Sahwit's Bracelet
Sahwit's bracelet is broken, and can no longer be detected by the prison's sensors.
↳Sahwit's bracelet wasn't working at the time of the incident, and could not be detected by the sensors.


Dog Chess Piece
Carved for fun by Kanis. He also seems to be engaged in correspondence chess via postal mail.


Prison Search
Winner, Gavèlle and the warden searched the prison for the murder weapon, but couldn't find anything.


Wiped Floor
There are signs that the floor in the victim's detention center cell was wiped clean.
↳There are signs that the floor in the victim's cell was wiped clean. I've ordered a test to see if it was blood.
↳There are signs that blood was wiped from the floor of the victim's cell. We're currently checking whose blood it is.
↳There are signs that blood was wiped from the floor of the victim's cell. The blood has been confirmed as Knight's.


Chess Diagram
The positions of the pieces match those on the board in Kanis's cell.
↳Knight was playing correspondence chess against Kanis. Did the two have some sort of connection?


Security Camera Footage
Footage of a detention center holding cell showing the victim being attacked by a dark form.
↳The time stamp is off due to a three-hour gap in the footage. This was actually recorded around 9 AM.


Missing Guard Uniform
Stolen from a guard's locker in the detention center corridor on March 26th.


Mr. Knight's Cell Key
A guard was attacked and his keys, including the one to Knight's cell, were stolen. They have yet to be recovered.
↳A guard was attacked the day before the murder, and his keys were stolen. They were found in Carcerato's possession.


Security Gates
Deployed at the entrances to both the detention center and the prison proper. Capable of detecting items made of metal.


Tunnel Prints
A photo of the tunnel between the prison and the detention center. It seems to depict the paw prints of a dog.
↳A photo of the tunnel between the prison and the detention center. Shows paw prints made by a bear.


Autopsy Report
The interim report suggests that the cause of death was a stab wound to the neck, inflicted by a sharp blade. Death was instantaneous.
↳Estimated time of death is between 3 AM and 6 AM on the 27th. Instant death from a stab wound to the neck.


Chisel
Contains traces of blood. Hidden by Kanis inside his dog's mouth. Has a small bell attached to it.
↳Belonged to Knight. Hidden by Kanis in Helmut's mouth in order to avoid suspicion.


Mr. Kanis's Bells
Only two such bells exist. One is attached to Helmut the dog, the other to Kanis's knife, which was seized by the police during his arrest.


Mr. Saint's Trick
A contraption that used the well, a very heavy weight, three pulleys, and two lengths of rope to send Saint flying during the show.
↳Used during the show to send Saint flying. The weights were replaced with the victim's body by someone.


Breakers
Located in the power room, just off the prison yard. Were they tampered with the night before the body was found?


Turnabout Legacy[]

Dec. 2000 evidence[]

Attorney's Badge
Proof of my profession. Polishing it every day has become just another part of my daily routine.


Seal
A seal that was found near the body when it was discovered. What could the design signify?


Crime Scene Notes
Notes about the crime scene. The police have taken custody of the body.


Mr. Tangaroa's Teapot
A Paul Halique piece with Tangaroa's fingerprints on it.


Contest Rules
A piece of paper listing the rules for the contest.


Frame Finger Marks
Someone left finger marks on the chocolate frame, but didn't leave any prints.


Mansion Key
The one and only key that unlocks every door in the mansion. Tangaroa was in possession of it the entire time.


Salt Lamp
Salt lamp found in Scone's room. Stained with the victim's blood.


Fake Confections
Ms. Scone's so-called creations were all fake plastic models, covered in a thick layer of whipped cream.


Luminous Cloth
Special color-changing cloth belonging to Scone. She has lost a roll of it, along with the device that powers it.


Chocolates
Tangaroa's favorite, handmade by Bound. Rather misshapened, and incredibly sweet.


Instant Camera
Takes photos that develop instantly. A new film cartridge has twenty exposures, but there are only three remaining.


Mr. Frost's Room
A photo of Frost's sculptures, taken by Judy Bound.
↳Two photos, one from Bound and one from Detective Badd.


Ms. Scone's Statement
Frost's sculptures were "scrumptious", except for the lyre, which was salty.


Semifinal Entries
Frost's and Gusto's creations had different designs, but tasted the same.


Ultimate Cookbook
A book containing formulas for creating a cure for taste disorders and other chemical and pharmaceutical items.


Victim's Blood
Frost's blood was found in the fountain, but not at the scene of the crime.


Family Photo
A photo of Frost, Gusto, and their sons. Gusto's son visited his father daily up through the semifinal.


Apr. 2019 evidence[]

Prosecutor's Badge
Proof of my profession. However, I prefer to keep it in my pocket.


IS-7 Incident Case File
Documents providing an overview of the IS-7 Incident.
↳Documents received from Fender, including his comments in red.


Gallery Pamphlet
A pamphlet received upon entry to the gallery.


Winter Wing Photo
Statues matching those made by the victim eighteen years ago.


Pisces Statue Sketch
A sketch by Larry depicting the Pisces statue exactly as he saw it.


Used Blowtorch
There are signs it was used just before the police arrived. Possibly to heat something?


Luminous Cloth
Cloth that glows when connected to a full-spectrum light-emitting device.
↳Delicia Scone's property. One of these was lost eighteen years ago.
↳Given to Bound by Scone. Lost eighteen years ago, only to be rediscovered floating in the fountain.


Poison Gas Ingredients
The gas was produced when bright red Ubiquium and pure white Asphyxion were mixed together.


Ultimate Cookbook
A book containing formulas for creating a cure for taste disorders and other chemical and pharmaceutical items.


Frame Finger Marks
Finger marks left by someone in Tangaroa's room. No accompanying prints.


Salt Lamp
A photograph of the murder weapon from the IS-7 Incident. It's a salt lamp stained with Frost's blood.


Family Photo
A photo of Frost, Gusto, and their sons. Gusto's son visited his father daily up through the semifinal.


Teapot
Designed by Paul Halique. One of Judy Bound's prize possessions.


Ceramic Fragment
Found in a puddle of tea. The design looks familiar.


Lift Cart
Used by Frost to transport ice eighteen years ago. The top of the platform is wet.


Larry's Sketches
Sketches depicting exactly what Larry saw.


Gemini Case Analysis Results
Results of the analysis conducted on the melted contents of the Gemini case.


Chocolates
Handmade by Bound. Slightly melted after being kept in her pocket.


Statute of Limitations
A book explaining the statutes of limitations of various crimes.


A Turnabout Forsaken[]

Prosecutor's Badge
Proof of my profession. However, I prefer to keep it in my pocket.


Yatagarasu Pin
The Yatagarasu pin that Kay always wears.


Jammin' Ninja Mask
A mask modeled on the Jammin' Ninja's face. Made of cheap plastic.


Letter
A letter Kay was carrying. It is addressed to her.


Promise Notebook
A book of promises Kay made to her father when she was a little girl. What was it doing in the Committee chamber?


Ticket Stub
Stub from a ticket granting entry to the viewing platform on top of the Bigg Building.


Magenta Flower
A magenta flower made of fabric that is usually worn on the chest as a decoration. Also known as a corsage.
↳A magenta flower made of fabric that is usually worn on the chest as a decoration. Torn from the victim's clothes.


Bigg Building Pamphlet
Contains a cross-section of the building and a plan of the viewing platform.


Kay's Recollections
Kay remembers a person coming from beyond the concession stand and pushing her off of the building.


Candelabrum
A three-armed candle holder that may have been the murder weapon, judging by the wounds to the the victim's chest.
↳A three-armed candle holder found by the victim's body. Made to look like the murder weapon.


Keycard
A keycard that grants entry to the Committee chamber. Found in the victim's hand.


Coroner's Findings → Autopsy Report
Initial findings of Dr. Hilda Hertz. Cause of death: Stab wounds to chest. Head injury sustained post mortem.
↳Death occurred as a result of a heavy blow to the head with a circular object. Chest wounds inflicted post-mortem.


Keycard Record
Record of keycards used to enter the Committee chamber.


Victim's Letter
A letter that appears to have been written to the victim by Kay.


Broken Plushie
The stuffed animal we originally found in the Zheng Fa presidential plane. Missing its left horn.
↳Fitted with a recording device that captured part of a conversation. One of the people recorded had a prominent burn.


Masks
A selection of various masks was kept on a rack. Each participant in the auction wore one.


Ringleader's Appearance
Ringleader wore a white jacket, white gloves, a magenta flower, and a mask. Age and gender could not be discerned.
↳Ringleader wore a white jacket, white gloves, a magenta flower, and a mask. Also had a tattoo on the part of their face the mask didn't cover.


Ms. Hart's Picture
A photograph of the mysterious figure in the red raincoat. Click here for details.


Ms. Hart's Statement
Ms. Hart's observations about what happened during the mysterious auction.


Elevator Blood
Blood was detected on the secret elevator. It looks like it dripped through to the floor below.


Clothes Box
Box in which the killer hid the body. Traces of blood were detected inside.


Committee Chamber Blood
Blood found in the Committee chamber. Was it placed there to hide the existence of the storeroom?


Red Raincoat
Worn by the victim, whose fingerprints and blood were found on the hood.


Nurse Niedler's Statement
Niedler's description of how she swapped places with Rosie Ringer.


IS-7 Incident Case File
Details of a murder case from eighteen years ago.


Turnabout for the Ages[]

Yatagarasu Pin
The Yatagarasu pin that Kay always wears.
↳The Yatagarasu pin that Kay always wears. There was a bug hidden inside.


Forged Letter
A letter written by an unknown person claiming to be Kay. Touch the Check Button for details.


Bigg Building
Site of the underground auctions. There is an entrance to the hidden fifty-first floor on the roof.


Creature Feature Flyer
Latest in the series after a twelve-year hiatus: "Taurusaurus Vs. Gourdy".


Movie Set Locks
The combination lock on the front gate was left untouched, while the side gate's chain was cut sometime last night.


Crime Scene Notes → President Wang's Autopsy Report
Surrounded by shards of concrete. Flecks of something yellow stuck to clothes. Touch the Check Button for details.


Monster Footprints
The concrete was broken and the earth exposed. But are they really footprints?
↳The concrete was broken and the earth exposed. Did Excelsius Winner dig up the ransom money?
↳The concrete was broken and the earth exposed after Excelsius Winner dug up President Wang's corpse.


Taurusaurus Head
Modeled on the first generation Taurusaurus design. Seems like it fell from the roof of the on-set production trailer last night.


Taurusaurus Plushie
Belonged to President Wang. It can record and play back sound.


Behind-the-Scenes Photo
Photo taken yesterday. No monster footprints and no body were visible at that point.


Security Camera Footage
Shows President Wang and Judge Gavèlle in front of the elevator on the roof of the Bigg Building.
↳Shows President Wang and Judge Gavèlle arriving on the roof of the Bigg Building. There's no footage of the president riding the elevator down to leave.


Ms. Hart's Statement
Ms. Hart was in the 51st-floor storeroom the night before last, but didn't see the president pass through.


Shaun's Rehearsal Tape
Filmed yesterday evening. Shows Shaun rehearsing by a monster footprint.


Blue Truck
Parked in front of the Bigg Building. Driver is nowhere to be found.


Mechanic's Gloves
Meant for motorcycle maintenance, but currently covered in dirt for some reason.
↳Excelsius Winner wore these when digging the monster footprints.


IS-7 Incident Case File
Details of a murder case from eighteen years ago. Touch the Check Button for details.


Report on Knight
A report from Warden Laguarde to Chairman Winner about the connection between Knight and Kanis.


Knight's Possessions
A pocket chess set and a ring. Found in the safe in the auction storeroom.
↳A pocket chess set and a ring Knight made from his father's signature stamp.


Bug
The person in the red raincoat planted this inside Kay's Yatagarasu pin.


Ms. Lloyd's Tape
A recording of signals that were intercepted from the bug that was planted in Kay's pin.


Bouquet
A bouquet of lion lilies given to President Wang by Judge Gavèlle. Recovered from on top of his body by Shaun.


Sedative
Used on both Kay and Shaun during their kidnapping. Brand name is "3 Zs".


SS-5 Incident Case File
12-year-old incident in which President Wang was kidnapped and the sole witness was murdered. Touch the Check Button for details.


Fire
One of the children set the orphanage yard on fire as a prank sometime during the evening before the incident.


Mr. Aldown's Autopsy Report
Victim was struck on the head and died at around 1 AM on February 10th.


Mr. Aldown's Final Call
Message left on Rosie Ringer's phone detailing what Aldown saw.


Bloody Button
Found clutched in Aldown's right hand. The blood is his.


Mr. Aldown's Photograph
Shows the president being kidnapped. Only one picture was taken. Touch the Check Button for details.
↳Staged photograph taken after Aldown was killed. Touch the Check Button for details.


Victim's Shoes
Not actually Aldown's shoes. They appear to have been placed on his feet by the culprit after the murder took place.


Mysterious Blood Stain
Left on the scene of the murder 12 years ago. The blood wasn't Aldown's.


Child's Drawing
A picture drawn by the child who caused the fire during the incident 12 years ago. It depicts what he saw the night of the incident.


Correspondence Chess Diagram
Sent to Bronco Knight by Bodhidharma Kanis. Printed rather than hand-written.
↳Letter from Kanis retyped by the mastermind, and then sent on to Bronco Knight.


Lion Hot Air Balloon
Smaller balloon used for promotional purposes. Saint is in charge of it and drives it around on the back of a truck.
