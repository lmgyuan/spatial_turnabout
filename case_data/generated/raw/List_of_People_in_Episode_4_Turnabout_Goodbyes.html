Christmas Eve: two men stand in a rowboat in the middle of a lake talking about their younger days. One says he has suffered for too long and, thus, wants revenge. He suddenly shoots the other man with a gun. The victim falls into the lake, as Miles Edgeworth leaves him to his watery grave. Wait! Miles Edgeworth?! The prosecutor? If he shot him, what did the other man do to him? What grudge did Edgeworth have against him? What kind of suffering did he go through? How can he possibly kill another man?

Case Information[edit | edit source]

  • Time of Crime: December 25, 12:15 AM
  • Location: Gourd Lake
  • Defendant: Miles Edgeworth
  • Cause of Death: Shot in the Heart
  • Murder Weapon: Pistol
  • Victim: Robert Hammond
  • Defense Attorney: Phoenix Wright
  • Prosecution Lawyer: Manfred von Karma

Characters[edit | edit source]

Defense Attorney[edit | edit source]

Phoenix Wright

In this episode, Phoenix will be defending his own rival, but can he win against a man with a winning streak ten times longer than Edgeworth's?

Defendant[edit | edit source]

Miles Edgeworth

Edgeworth is Phoenix’s rival. He became a prosecutor at the age of 20. He has a profound hatred for crime and criminals. It seems now he'll know how it feels to be called a criminal.

Victim[edit | edit source]

Robert Hammond

Not much is known about Robert Hammond or his connection to Edgeworth.

Prosecution Lawyer[edit | edit source]

Manfred von Karma

A prosecutor to truly fear, Von Karma has never lost a case in 40 years, or at least claims to. Used to be Edgeworth's mentor.

Witnesses[edit | edit source]

Lotta Hart

Lotta was camping at the lake on the night of the murder. She is the main witness to the murder. She claims that she was researching meteor showers on the lake that included using a camera triggered by loud noises.

The Boat Shop Caretaker

An old man who lives at the Boat Shop on Gourd Lake. Apparently, he seems to have completely lost his memory and no one knows his real name or any other information about him.

Other characters[edit | edit source]

Maya Fey

Maya is Phoenix's helpful assistant. Months with no training seems to have drained her medium powers.

Detective Dick Gumshoe

Gumshoe is absent-minded and can be short-tempered at times. Mostly he is a friendly and helpful character. He has great respect for Miles Edgeworth.

Larry Butz

Larry is Phoenix's best friend from grade school. As of this case, Larry works at the Samurai Dog stand on the beach.

Marvin Grossberg

Marvin Grossberg seems to know something about this case.

Gregory Edgeworth

Gregory Edgeworth is Miles Edgeworth's deceased father.

Polly

Polly is the caretaker's only companion.

Yanni Yogi

The suspect of the DL-6 Incident. His wherebout is unknown.

Locations[edit | edit source]

  • Wright & Co. Law Offices
    This is where you work.

  • Detention Cell Visitor's Room
    This is where the defendant (in this case Edgeworth) is held.

  • Gourd Lake Entrance
    This is the official Gourd Lake Entrance.

  • Gourd Lake Public Beach
    This is where most people go when they visit Gourd Lake.

  • Boat Rental Shop
    This is where people rent boats to explore the lake.

  • Caretaker's Shack
    This is where the Boat Shop caretaker lives and works. He mistakes it for a Noodle Shop for some reason.

  • Gourd Lake Woods
    This is where Lotta claims to have witnessed the murder, but what was she doing camping there?

  • Police Department Criminal Affairs
    This is where detectives, such as Gumshoe, work.

  • Police Department Records Room
    This is where evidence from solved or unsolved cases is held.
